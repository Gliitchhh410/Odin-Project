Margherita Pizza Recipe

Margherita Pizza is a traditional Italian pizza topped with fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. It’s a simple yet flavorful pizza that highlights the freshness of its ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Arrange slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil. Slice and serve.
