Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish with alternating layers of pasta sheets, a rich meat or vegetable sauce, ricotta cheese mixture, and mozzarella, baked until bubbly and golden . It’s a comforting, hearty meal that’s perfect for feeding a crowd .

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Onion, diced
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Egg
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook ground beef or Italian sausage with diced onion and minced garlic until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, chopped parsley or basil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Sprinkle a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
