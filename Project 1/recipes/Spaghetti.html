Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It’s a rich and creamy dish that’s both simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon, diced
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions. Reserve some pasta water, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, cook diced pancetta or bacon until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta. Toss to combine.
  5. Quickly add the egg and cheese mixture to the pasta, tossing continuously to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Season with freshly ground black pepper and serve immediately.
